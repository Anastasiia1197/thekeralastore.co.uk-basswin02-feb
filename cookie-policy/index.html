Cookie Policy – Designed with You in Mind

This Cookie Policy explains how https://www.benchtop.co.nz/ ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. Our primary goal is to provide you with a seamless, secure, and personalized online experience while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and transparency. By understanding and using these technologies responsibly, we can enhance site functionality, analyze traffic patterns, and deliver relevant content. This policy is an integral part of our broader Privacy Policy and should be read in conjunction with it.

Unlocking better experiences: How cookies work

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide valuable information to the site owners. On our platform, cookies enable essential functions such as maintaining your logged-in session, remembering your game preferences and language settings, and keeping items in your virtual cart during a bonus claim process. Without certain cookies, core features of the website would not operate correctly.

Data we collect and our strict commitment to responsible use

The data collected via cookies is used strictly to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our service. This may include non-personally identifiable information such as IP address, browser type, device characteristics, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral sources. We use this aggregated data to understand how our site is used, identify areas for technical improvement, and ensure our promotional content is relevant to our New Zealand audience. We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information without your explicit consent.

Third-party services: Ensuring safe and transparent tracking

We engage with reputable third-party service providers who may also set cookies on our behalf. These partners assist us with critical functions such as website analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), performance monitoring, fraud prevention, and advertising campaign measurement. These third parties are contractually bound to use the information only for the purposes we have specified and in accordance with applicable data protection laws. Their privacy policies are available on their respective websites, and we encourage you to review them.

Take control: How to customize your cookie preferences easily

You have full control over the use of cookies. Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies, to accept cookies only from trusted sites, or to alert you each time a website tries to place a cookie. You can also delete cookies that have already been stored on your device. Please be aware that if you choose to disable or block certain cookies, some features of https://www.benchtop.co.nz/ may not function properly, and your user experience may be impacted. For detailed instructions on managing cookies, please consult your browser's "Help" section.
